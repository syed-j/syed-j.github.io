Insights & Outcomes

67% Revenue Growth (2019–2021)

Revenue surged from $25.9M to $43.4M over 3 years, with acceleration in 2021 (+39% YoY) driven by post-pandemic demand in materials processing and research markets.

Germany + US = 80% of Revenue

Two divisions account for $80.7M of $100.6M total. The remaining 4 divisions (UK, India, Russia, Korea) collectively contribute just 19.8%, signaling heavy geographic concentration risk.

Materials Processing Dominates

At $47.5M (47.3%), Materials Processing generates nearly half of all revenue — more than Telecom and Medical combined. General Manufacturing and Research are the top two end markets.

Medical Segment Underrepresented

Medical accounts for only 7.2% ($7.2M) despite strong industry growth. This represents a potential expansion opportunity given the company's existing product capabilities.
